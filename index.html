I’ll See You in the Sky simulates the color of the sky base on your location (city's name) input, creating a quiet little corner on the Interweb. I’ll See You in the Sky invites you to choose where to go so we can watch the color of the sky together and just breath.

The base color is generated by the current time of the location, and is saturated by the warm or cool tone based on the location temperature. The location’s weather conditions (rain, snow, cloudy, drizzle, thunderstorm) will further saturate the base color. The wind directional angle determines the angle of the gradient.

I’ll See You in the Sky is developed by Panharith Ean, using weather API from OpenWeather
